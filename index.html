Create. Learn. Play.




Learn how to Build and Program your own Robot

We make it easy for you to build your own robot, program its behaviours, and see it working live in just a few minutes! Robots are the future!


Dexter is a mobile companion that teaches the fundamentals of programming, logic, and robot-building in a fun and interactive way. Dexter starts off knowing nothing about the world, and gazes blankly, devoid of intelligence and emotion. It is up to you to give him intelligence, personality and emotions!

Using our simple Scratch-inspired block programming language, Dexter can be programmed to interact however you want with the world.





Dexter is simply a mobile app, so it is up to you to build a body suitable for your Dexter. We support the most popular and affordable hardware platforms. Dexter can directly connect with Lego Mindstorms NXT/EV3, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi.
