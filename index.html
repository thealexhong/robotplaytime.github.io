Learn how to Build and Program your own Robot

Our platform enables anyone to learn the fundamentals of robotics - from building hardware to writing code. We make it easy for anyone to build their own robot, code its behaviours, and see it working live within a few minutes! Robots are the future!

Dexter

Dexter is a mobile companion that teaches the fundamentals of programming, logic, and robot-building in a fun and interactive way. Dexter starts off knowing nothing about the world, and gazes blankly, devoid of intelligence and emotion. It is up to you to give him personality and emotions!

Using our simple Scratch-inspired block programming language, Dexter can be programmed to interact with the world. Its intelligence is up to you to decide!


Dexter is simply a mobile app, so it is up to you to build a body suitable for your Dexter. We support the most popular and affordable hardware platforms. Dexter can directly connect with Lego Mindstorms NXT/EV3, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi.
